STATE-SPECIFIC ADDENDUM — DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
Under District of Columbia insurance law and the District of Columbia Unfair Claims Settlement
Practices Act, you have the right to a timely investigation, a written
explanation of any adverse decision, and to dispute factual statements
relied upon by the insurer.

To file a complaint or request assistance, contact the District of Columbia Department
of Insurance via your state's official consumer-protection website. Most
state departments accept complaints by phone, mail, or online portal.

You may submit additional information to the carrier address above for
reconsideration of this decision.
