Section 06A · Agent A11

Total-Loss & Salvage Orchestrator

How A11 fits the pipeline
A11 runs automatically whenever Stage S4B (AI Damage Assessment) flags a vehicle as a total loss. It applies the state-specific Total-Loss Threshold (TLT), refines the ACV with mileage / condition / options adjustments, recommends a branded title (SALVAGE · NON-REPAIRABLE · FLOOD · HAIL), shadow-quotes Copart and IAA, and produces two settlement options (carrier-retains vs owner-retains salvage). The LLM only drafts the customer notification letter — every dollar figure is deterministic.
Evaluations
No total-loss evaluations yet. Submit a claim with high damage relative to ACV (try preset 3 — "Total loss") to generate one.
Evaluation Detail
Select an evaluation on the left to view ACV breakdown, settlement options, salvage assignment, and the customer notification letter.
Section 06B

Salvage Vendor Network

Auto vendor selection
When vendor=auto, A11 runs shadow quotes against every configured vendor (Copart, IAA, Mock) and picks the highest expected net return for this specific vehicle, damage profile, and zip code. The carrier keeps full visibility — all losing quotes are recorded on the assignment.
